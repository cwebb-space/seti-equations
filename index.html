The Drake Equation & Its Variants

An interactive explorer of ten formulations — from Frank Drake's 1961 conference agenda to Bayesian reanalyses of the twenty-first century. Adjust the sliders to see how estimates of N change with your assumptions.

Prepared for a workshop on the history of SETI. Each equation reflects the scientific priorities and uncertainties of its moment. The parameters are illustrative; consult the cited sources for full quantitative treatments.
